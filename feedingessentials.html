Shopping Anyone?

My mom loves to shop, so she enjoyed trying out things that could possibly help me on my food journey. My dad wasn't too impressed with all the packages arriving at the door, but how can you say no to me? Here are some of the things that works well for me, as well a video review of some of these items.

  • Pretty in Pink Mama Review

    Here's a mother and son video reviewing some feeding essentials.

  • Sippy Cups
    My mom swears by the brand Mam. These cups are great practice cups, since they don't leak when I decide to drop them from the high chair.
  • Bibs
    It's going to be very messy in the beginning... although I'm still quite the messy eater. These bibs are great as they can catch some of the food for seconds, and are really easy to clean.
  • Suction Bowls
    My mom loves these bowls since they stick on top of the high chair table. They make it difficult for me to pick up and throw, but it doesn't mean I won't try.
  • Easymats
    These mats also stick really well onto the table top of my high chair. They have different compartments so that my food doesn't mix together. They are also really good for when we travel, as my mom just rolls it up.
  • Oxo Spoons
    These are really cool spoons. The shape of the spoon makes it really easy for me to eat the food my mom feeds me. It also comes in a nice case, so when I'm done eating, it get's put away easily.
