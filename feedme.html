Where to Begin?

There's two very different styles of feeding out there: Baby Led Weaning and the traditional pureed foods. No one says you have to do 100% of either method, it's entirely up to you. My mom chose to do 50% solid food, and 50% pureed food. Here's some of the foods my mom fed me that I love, and think you will too.

  • Pinwheels
    Pinwheels are delicious and super easy to make. My mom cheats and buys the pre-made puff pastry from the store and just rolls it out. There are so many variations tnat can be made, so it's just a matter of finding what ingredients you like best inside a pinwheel. Mine is pesto and mozarella cheese! There's loads of recipes on pinterest if you want some inspiration.
  • Brocolli
    Green veggies like brocolli are super healthy and yummy. I can't get enough of brocolli since I love being able to easily grab the stems and feed myself.
  • Fritters
    Fritters are also a super easy and yummy food for new eaters and experienced eaters like me. My mom tries to hide veggies inside these fritters by blending them first. Whether or not the veggies are blended in, it's still quite tasty!
  • Pouches
    Pouches are super easy and convenient for when we are out and about, or when mom just doesn't feel like cooking. There's so many flavours and they are great when trying get a variety of foods in the diet, as they are all blended together.
  • Jars
    Jars are super easy and convenient for when we are out and about as well, or when mom just doesn't feel like cooking. It's also super fun to try and feed myself. My mom enjoys cleaning me up afterwards!
