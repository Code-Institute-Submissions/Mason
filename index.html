My Journey

It's very scary in the beginning for my mom, as she didn't know whether she should pursue the Baby-Led Weaning method or go the old fashioned route with Purees. She read and read and read so many blogs, articles, and books, and in the end, realized that it didn't matter. It's what's best for her and me. You see, I didn't have any teeth until almost 11 months old, so my mom was terrified of the possibility of me choking. Although I did give her a few scares with some gagging in the beginning, she learned to relax (a little bit), and to just trust that whichever method she chose to feed me that day, I will be ok. Hopefully you will find the information here helpful.
